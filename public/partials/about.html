Why Atristic Collab?

  • Artistic Collab is a project where each user can get a chance to upload their own creatation.
  • Rather than just exhibiting and admiring each others work as isolated individuals, people can get gather to work on projects.
  • Some of them may have some interesting Music created ,painting, Video or just a poem or an article.
  • Other people can watch, like comment these artworks.
  • Everyone gets a customized feed of these artworks if they follow other users.
  • They can see the liked artworks in their own page whenever they want.

It's All about sharing the ideas! :)
